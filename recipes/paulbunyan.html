Paul Bunyan Recipe

Description: Example of Paul Bunyan Breakfast - Follow this Recipe for Much More Appetizing Results!!

Ingredients

  • Bacon, Lots of Bacon!!
  • Eggs(3 Per Person Eating)
  • Chopped Potatoes(Lazy People may use Ore-Ida's "Potatoes O'Brien")
  • Onions(Sliced or Diced to Personal Preference)
  • Peppers(Potatoes O'Brien users may skip this ingredient)
  • Salt, Pepper, and Emril's Seasoning
  • (optional)Mushrooms
  • (optional)Cilantro

Steps

  1. Cook Bacon to liking. When complete, place bacon on a plate but leave bacon grease in the frying pan.
  2. Pour Chopped Potatoes and Peppers into Bacon grease and cook until potatoes are browned.
  3. Add Eggs, Onions, Mushrooms, and Salt, Pepper, and Emril's Seasoning until Eggs are 75% Cooked
  4. Add Bacon back into the pan and mix well
  5. Add Cilantro and Serve!
