Lasagna Recipe

Description: Scrumptious Lasagna Cooked Following This Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Italian Sausage
  • Lean Ground Beed
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 Can Crushed Tomatoes
  • 2 Cans Tomato Sauce
  • 2 Cans Tomato Paste
  • 2 Tablespoons of White Sugar
  • Seasonings: Parsely, Dried Basil Leaves, Salt, Italian Seasoning, Fennel Seeds, Black Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Parmesean, Mozzarella, and Ricotta Cheese
  • 1 Egg

Steps

  1. Make the Meat Sauce: cook meats and add tomato products, onions, garlic, seasonings, and sugar
  2. Cook the Noodles
  3. Mix the Ricotta: add egg to liming oozing
  4. Layer the Lasagna: SEE BELOW INSTRUCTIONS FOR LAYERING
  5. Cover with Foil and Bake
  6. Let Lasagna Rest Before Serving

How To Layer Lasagna

  1. Meat Sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta Cheese Mix
  4. Mozzarella Slices
  5. Meat Sauce
  6. Parmesean Cheese
  7. Repeat Steps and Top with Remaining Parmesean
